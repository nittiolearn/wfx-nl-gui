Define the course properties

  • Title: Mandatory - enter a title for your training.
  • From:Select the start date for your training.
  • Till:Select the end date for your training.
  • Feedback form or module: Mandatory - Select the feedback form or the learning module for your training by clicking on the search icon.
  • Description: Provide a short description which will help others in the group to understand the purpose of this training.
