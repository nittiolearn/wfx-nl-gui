Approved modules are visible to users who have permission to view approved content. These are typically two kind of users who get to view the approved modules:
  • Learners who self learn from the knowledge repository/library.
  • Advanced users who assign learning modules to other learners.
You may restrict the access to a subset of users using filters available below. If no filter is chosen, the module will be accessible to all users who have permission to view approved content.
If one or more of the filters are selected, only users with permission to view approved content and belong to the selected filters will be allowed to access the module.
Note 1: If you filter out your own organization, you will not be able to use the module for sending it out as an assignment.
Note 2: If a module is assigned to a user, it is accessible to that user even if the module is not approved for the user's organization. The filters chosen while approving a module controls only the set of users who can access the module without the module being explicilty assigned to them.
visibility: Depending on your group type, you would have option to make the content available to other groups. By default the content is only available to users within the group.
Once you approve, this module will be visible to other users who have permission to view approved modules. They will be able to assign the module to other learners within your organization or self learn depending on their user rights.
Cick on help button above to see how to control access to your module.
